Sate Ayam Madura (Madura-Style Chicken Satay)


Photo by Timolina on Freepik

Description:

Sate Ayam Madura is a famous Indonesian grilled chicken dish from Madura Island. Pieces of marinated chicken are skewered and grilled over charcoal, then served with a rich, sweet, and savory peanut sauce made with roasted peanuts and sweet soy sauce. Itâ€™s smoky, juicy, and deeply flavorful.

Ingredients:

For the chicken:

  • 500 g boneless chicken thigh or breast, diced
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Bamboo skewers

Peanut Sauce:

  • 150 g roasted peanuts (or 3 tbsp peanut butter)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 red chilies (adjust to spice level)
  • 2 tbsp palm sugar or brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 200 ml water

Garnish (optional)

  • Fried shallots
  • Lime wedges
  • Lontong (rice cakes) or steamed rice

Instructions:

  1. Marinate Chicken:

    • Mix chicken with soy sauce, lime juice, and oil.
    • Let it marinate for 30 minutes or overnight.
    • Skewer the chicken onto bamboo sticks.

  2. Prepare Peanut Sauce:

    • Blend peanuts, garlic, and chilies with a little water.
    • Cook the mixture in a pan, then add sugar, soy sauce, tamarind, salt, and the rest of the water.
    • Simmer until thick and smooth.

  3. Grill:

    • Grill satay over charcoal or pan, basting with sweet soy sauce until charred.

  4. Serve:

    • Top with peanut sauce, garnish with fried shallots, and enjoy with lontong or rice.
